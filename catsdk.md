## 📦 What is CatSDK?

**CatSDK** is a complete creative toolkit for building:
- **Emulators** (Game Boy, NES, CHIP-8, etc.)
- **ROMs & homebrew games**
- **Game engines** (2D/retro focused)
- **Creative coding experiments**

Born from the **Blue Hue** emulator project, CatSDK provides reusable patterns, utilities, and inspiration for anyone wanting to craft their own retro gaming tools.

---

## 🧠 Philosophy

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Single-file first** | One script, no project boilerplate |
| **Auto-magic dependencies** | Self-installing imports |
| **Retro aesthetic** | Black bg, electric blue (#33ccff) — *the CatSDK signature* |
| **Play > Perfection** | Ship it, break it, fix it, repeat |
| **Creative freedom** | No locked-in frameworks—use what you love |
